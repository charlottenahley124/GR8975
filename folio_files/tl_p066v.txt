To make or mat beautiful, put in a little varnish or fatty oil, not the kind that is made from cleaning brushes, but the pure kind, that is made fatty in the sun, or mixed with ceruse.

 

Or mat of this kind is scarcely less beautiful than burnished es gold, and lasts longer in the rain &amp; the like, &amp; is made more quickly. Burnished gold cracks with time and comes apart in the rain.
