## To make varnish

 
Take one pound of linseed oil, and then put it in a pot of earth mixed with a breadcrust and three onions, and put it on a charcoal fire, and you will cook it on a low fire, and let it boil for the space of five hours. You will take half an ounce of flour glue, and will make it boil just as before, and stir continuously with a spoon.
